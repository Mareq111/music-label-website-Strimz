Tour - 2024

Title: Red Path To Zukunft - Tour

May - October 2024 Live Shows Across Europe


The 'Red Path to Zukunft' tour, literally 'Red Path to the Future,' is a musical journey in which the artist tells the story of his choice to learn programming as his own main path to discovering a more exciting future.


Finding one's 'Red Path' can be the key to creating a future full of satisfaction and fulfillment, according to the upcoming album of the same name.
