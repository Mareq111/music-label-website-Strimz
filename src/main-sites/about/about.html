Strimz Empire

It's the label

Origins

The music label Strimz Empire was started by underground artist El Double M. He aims to share our music for free and unite fellow artists within the label.


El Double M established the label in 2018 to release and promote his own music, culminating in his debut album Initial Confusion . Before he made the album, he put out some songs on YouTube to get more people interested and follow together with him.

Main Idea

Strimz Empire isn't just a label — it's a supportive space for artists to explore and grow. We're all about embracing diverse creativity. We work closely with artists, not only helping them create music but also supporting their personal and professional development. We're here to lay a strong foundation for their artistic progress.


We enables free access to our music through streaming platforms and a built-in player on our website. Our aim is to allow listeners the freedom to enjoy selected albums and singles from our label without any charges. Through this initiative, we promote our artists and their creative works, facilitating free access to their creations.


Moreover, for those keen on directly supporting the artists, we offer the option to purchase albums, event tickets, unique merchandise related to our artists, and various other appealing products through the Strimz Shop . This form of support through acquiring their works allows artists to develop independently and create art while enabling fans to be part of their creative journey.
