La Triple C

Musician, producer, and founder of Strimz Empire.

La Triple C, also known as Mikez Jacks, is a versatile music creator. With 8 albums and multiple singles released, his latest deluxe edition album, Flashback from 2001, is a nostalgic tribute to the vibrant essence of the 00s era, blending it seamlessly with contemporary elements.


His music resonates with emotion and bold experimentation, making him an influential figure shaping today's music scene. He is always open to groundbreaking sounds and emerging artists.

Jackie Ras

Southern-rooted rock enthusiast, love create storytelling songs.

Since joining our label in 2021, Jackie Ras has released the solo album Lone Rider and a collaborative album with Molly Granoli titled Minutes To Midnight.


His music takes listeners on a journey through Southern roots, crafting beautiful songs that resonate with people all over the world. Future projects promise even greater innovation and emotions that will move and delight everyone.

El Kaptn

Blending the best of wave music and country rap.

El Kaptn, also known as Marquez Kaptns, joined Strimz Empire in 2022. He started his musical journey with us by releasing the single Career Starvation. This song caught people's attention and led to more singles, creating excitement around his music.


Finally in 2023, Marquez shared his first album Pull Up with the world, expressing his artistic vision. His music has evolved with a mix of different sounds, offering intense experiences for listeners exploring his creative style.

Molly Granoli

Deeply passionate about wave music and also futuristic retro synth sounds.

Molly Granoli, the first artist to join our label at the beginning of 2021, has released several albums, including a remarkable collaborative album with Jackie Ras titled Minutes To Midnight.


This collaborative effort seamlessly merges intriguing synth and rock sounds with various abstract styles. Molly persistently explores unconventional musical realms, continuously experimenting with innovative tones and compositions, defining her unique artistic journey.
