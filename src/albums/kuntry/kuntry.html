Album Introduction

Fasten your seatbelts and get ready for an extraordinary journey through the heart of Mazovian countryside with El Double M album Kuntry.


This travel inspired album reflects diverse facets of life. We embark prepared for new challenges, learning to embrace fate, pursuing dreams, and experiencing changes and hardships, ultimately finding tranquility in life's simplicity.
